### SLIDE 9: CROATIA - GASTRONOMY TOURISM ANALYSIS

**Title:** Croatia: Gastronomy as Primary Tourism Driver

**Key Points:**
- Mediterranean cuisine with regional diversity
- Key products: Olive oil, wine (130+ indigenous varieties), truffles, pršut
- Strong value chain: Local farms → restaurants → tourists
- International brand recognition (Michelin coverage)
- Challenge: Extreme seasonality (60%+ arrivals in July-August)

**Purpose:** Analyze Croatia's gastronomy tourism characteristics.

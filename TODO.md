## üìå KEY COMPARATIVE THEMES

1. **Tourism Dependency**
   - Croatia: HIGH (17-19% of GDP)
   - Russia: LOW (<0.5% of GDP)

2. **Economic Model**
   - Croatia: Tourism-oriented economy
   - Russia: Diversified industrial economy

3. **Gastronomy Role**
   - Croatia: Primary tourism driver, international brand
   - Russia: Cultural asset, domestic consumption focus

4. **Market Focus**
   - Croatia: International tourists (EU markets)
   - Russia: Domestic tourism (vast internal market)

5. **Value Chain Integration**
   - Croatia: Strong agriculture-tourism linkages
   - Russia: Developing linkages, regional variation

6. **Branding Effectiveness**
   - Croatia: Strong international recognition
   - Russia: Limited international gastronomy brand
